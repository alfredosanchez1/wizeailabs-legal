Privacy Policy

CaloriSnap by WizeAI Labs

Last updated: January 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

CaloriSnap collects the following information:

  • Account information: Email, username, and password when you register
  • Food photos: Images you take to analyze calories
  • Nutrition data: Information about calories and nutrients from your meals
  • Location: To determine your timezone and automatically categorize meals
  • Usage data: How you use the application to improve our services
  • Apple Health data (with your permission): Steps, calories burned, body weight, and nutritional data synchronized with Apple Health

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide calorie and nutrition analysis
  • Improve the accuracy of our food recognition algorithms
  • Personalize your experience in the application
  • Send notifications about your nutritional progress
  • Provide customer support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who help us operate the application (such as AI image analysis)

Third-Party Services We Use

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered image analysis
  • Supabase: For secure data storage
  • Apple: For payment processing (In-App Purchases)
  • Apple Health: For synchronizing health and nutrition data on your device (data stays on your device and iCloud)

4. Apple Health Integration

CaloriSnap integrates with Apple Health to provide a complete health tracking experience. This integration is optional and requires your explicit permission.

Data We Read from Apple Health:

  • Daily steps
  • Active and resting calories burned
  • Body weight

Data We Write to Apple Health:

  • Calories consumed from analyzed meals
  • Protein, carbohydrates, and fats
  • Fiber and sugars

How This Data Is Used:

This data is used exclusively to calculate your daily caloric balance and display personalized statistics within the app. Apple Health data is never shared with third parties or stored on our servers - it remains on your device and in your iCloud Health account.

User Control:

You can enable or disable Apple Health synchronization at any time from the "My Profile" section in the app. You can also manage CaloriSnap's access to Apple Health in your device's Settings > Health > Data Access.

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Regular security monitoring
  • Secure storage on protected servers

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Access your personal information
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data in a structured format
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@wizeailabs.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as you maintain an active account or as needed to provide our services. You can request account deletion at any time.

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except for information we must retain by law.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

CaloriSnap uses local storage technologies to:

  • Keep your session active
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve application performance
  • Analyze application usage

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Update of the "Last updated" date

We recommend reviewing this policy regularly.

10. Children's Privacy

CaloriSnap is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13.

If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us at: privacy@wizeailabs.com

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure that these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us:

  • Privacy email: privacy@wizeailabs.com
  • Support: support@wizeailabs.com
  • Company: WizeAI Labs
  • Website: https://wizeailabs.com

Política de Privacidad

CaloriSnap by WizeAI Labs

Última actualización: 8 de enero de 2026

1. Información que recopilamos

CaloriSnap recopila la siguiente información:

  • Información de la cuenta: Email, nombre de usuario y contraseña cuando te registras
  • Fotos de comidas: Las imágenes que tomas para analizar calorías
  • Datos de nutrición: Información sobre calorías y nutrientes de tus comidas
  • Ubicación: Para determinar la zona horaria y clasificar automáticamente las comidas
  • Datos de uso: Cómo usas la aplicación para mejorar nuestros servicios
  • Datos de Apple Health (con tu permiso): Pasos, calorías quemadas, peso corporal y datos nutricionales sincronizados con Apple Health

2. Cómo usamos tu información

Utilizamos tu información para:

  • Proporcionar análisis de calorías y nutrición
  • Mejorar la precisión de nuestros algoritmos de reconocimiento de alimentos
  • Personalizar tu experiencia en la aplicación
  • Enviar notificaciones sobre tu progreso nutricional
  • Proporcionar soporte al cliente

3. Compartir información

No vendemos tu información personal a terceros.

Podemos compartir tu información solo en estos casos:

  • Con tu consentimiento explícito
  • Para cumplir con obligaciones legales
  • Con proveedores de servicios que nos ayudan a operar la aplicación (como análisis de imágenes con IA)

Servicios de Terceros que Usamos

  • OpenAI: Para análisis de imágenes con IA
  • Supabase: Para almacenamiento seguro de datos
  • Apple: Para procesamiento de pagos (In-App Purchases)
  • Apple Health: Para sincronizar datos de salud y nutrición en tu dispositivo (los datos permanecen en tu dispositivo e iCloud)

4. Integración con Apple Health

CaloriSnap se integra con Apple Health para proporcionarte una experiencia completa de seguimiento de salud. Esta integración es opcional y requiere tu permiso explícito.

Datos que leemos de Apple Health:

  • Pasos diarios
  • Calorías quemadas (activas y en reposo)
  • Peso corporal

Datos que escribimos en Apple Health:

  • Calorías consumidas de las comidas analizadas
  • Proteínas, carbohidratos y grasas
  • Fibra y azúcares

Cómo se usan estos datos:

Estos datos se utilizan exclusivamente para calcular tu balance calórico diario y mostrarte estadísticas personalizadas dentro de la app. Los datos de Apple Health nunca se comparten con terceros ni se almacenan en nuestros servidores - permanecen en tu dispositivo y en tu cuenta de iCloud Health.

Control del usuario:

Puedes habilitar o deshabilitar la sincronización con Apple Health en cualquier momento desde la sección "Mi Perfil" de la app. También puedes gestionar el acceso de CaloriSnap a Apple Health en Ajustes > Salud > Acceso a datos de tu dispositivo.

5. Seguridad de datos

Implementamos medidas de seguridad técnicas y organizativas para proteger tu información personal, incluyendo:

  • Encriptación de datos en tránsito y en reposo
  • Acceso restringido a información personal

  • Almacenamiento seguro en servidores protegidos
